I draw characters — heroes, monsters, and the worlds they live in.

My work spans character design, concept sheets, finished illustration, and animation — rooted in anime and manga, but built for any story that needs a strong, memorable cast.

I like moving between worlds: gothic, cyberpunk, fantasy, folklore. Whatever the brief, I take a character the whole way — from the first silhouette to a fully rendered piece, and sometimes into motion.

Beyond the Canvas

About

I'm José Antonio Taborda Morán (JATM) — an artist based in Barcelona.

I work in an anime- and manga-rooted style, but I like jumping between worlds — gothic, cyberpunk, fantasy, folklore — and between disciplines: character design, illustration, concept art, comics, and animation. Whatever the story needs, that's where I go.

I came to art the long way around and started drawing seriously in 2022, with one rule: make the work I actually want to make. ART3XILE grew out of that — a name about leaving home and finding it again in the work.

What I love most is a new character to figure out: who they are, how they move, what they carry, and the story only they can tell.

My work spans character design, concept sheets, finished illustration, and animation — rooted in anime and manga, but built for any story that needs a strong, memorable cast.

I like moving between worlds: gothic, cyberpunk, fantasy, folklore. Whatever the brief, I take a character the whole way — from the first silhouette to a fully rendered piece, and sometimes into motion.

About

I'm José Antonio Taborda Morán (JATM) — an artist based in Barcelona.

I work in an anime- and manga-rooted style, but I like jumping between worlds — gothic, cyberpunk, fantasy, folklore — and between disciplines: character design, illustration, concept art, comics, and animation. Whatever the story needs, that's where I go.

I came to art the long way around and started drawing seriously in 2022, with one rule: make the work I actually want to make. ART3XILE grew out of that — a name about leaving home and finding it again in the work.

What I love most is a new character to figure out: who they are, how they move, what they carry, and the story only they can tell.

My work spans character design, concept sheets, finished illustration, and animation — rooted in anime and manga, but built for any story that needs a strong, memorable cast.

I like moving between worlds: gothic, cyberpunk, fantasy, folklore. Whatever the brief, I take a character the whole way — from the first silhouette to a fully rendered piece, and sometimes into motion.

About

I'm José Antonio Taborda Morán (JATM) — an artist based in Barcelona.

I work in an anime- and manga-rooted style, but I like jumping between worlds — gothic, cyberpunk, fantasy, folklore — and between disciplines: character design, illustration, concept art, comics, and animation. Whatever the story needs, that's where I go.

I came to art the long way around and started drawing seriously in 2022, with one rule: make the work I actually want to make. ART3XILE grew out of that — a name about leaving home and finding it again in the work.

What I love most is a new character to figure out: who they are, how they move, what they carry, and the story only they can tell.
